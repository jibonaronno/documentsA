This is slot drawing in the sketcher workbench. I wanted to make symmetric the 2 center points of the slot with respect to the X axis or center origin. First draw the slot out of the axis line, then select the 2 center points and the X axis, then click "Symmetric Constraint" button.
Then select any of the 2 center points and select the Y axis and click Coincident constraint
